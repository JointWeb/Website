Lenten & Easter Services


Wednesday, February 14, Ash Wednesday, we will begin our Community Lenten services. There will be an afternoon service at 2:00PM and a 7:00Pm service, at St. John's Lutheran Church, 222 South Tulpehocken Street, Pine Grove. Included within the services will be the imposition of ashes and Holy Communion.


Wednesday, February 21, is the start of the Midweek Lenten services. This year's theme: At the Name of Jesus. The theme is based on the scripture passage from Philippians 2:5-11. There will be an afternoon service at 2:00PM. In the evening at 7:00Pm will be Holden Evening Prayer service. The Midweek Lenten services will be held on the following dates: February 28, March 6, 13, 20. All services will be at St. Johnâ€™s Lutheran Church, 222 South Tulpehocken Street, Pine Grove. Thursday, March 28, at 2:00PM and 7:00PM will be Maundy Thursday services. The service will include Holy Communion.


Friday, March 29, at 2:00PM and 7:00PM will be Good Friday services. The evening service will be a Tenebrae service (Service of the Shadows). Both Holy Week services will be held at St. John's Lutheran Church. All offering gifts will be designated for Pine Grove Area Council of Churches food ministry programs, and the ELCA World Hunger Ministry. Everyone is welcome to join us in worship as we gather as community.
